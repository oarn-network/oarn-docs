## 2. Entity Structure

### Option A: No Legal Entity (True Decentralization)
- [ ] Fully decentralized from day 1
- [ ] Contributors are individuals, not employees
- [ ] DAO is sole governance structure
- [ ] Pros: No regulatory target
- [ ] Cons: Harder to sign contracts, receive grants

### Option B: Foundation (Non-Profit)
- [ ] Swiss Foundation (Stiftung)
- [ ] Cayman Islands Foundation Company
- [ ] Purpose: Steward protocol development
- [ ] Sunset clause (dissolve when DAO mature)

### Option C: Hybrid
- [ ] Foundation for initial development
- [ ] DAO for ongoing governance
- [ ] Clear transition plan

### Tasks

- [ ] Engage formation lawyers
- [ ] Draft governing documents
- [ ] Appoint directors/supervisors
- [ ] Open bank accounts (if applicable)
